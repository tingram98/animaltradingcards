Dog

Dogs' sense of smell is at least 40 times better than humans.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus Familiaris
  • Average Body Temp: 101.0 to 102.5 F
  • Average Lifespan: 10-13 years
  • Habitat: Safe Homes

A dog is a domestic mammal of the Canidae family and the Carnivora order. Dogs are susbspecies of gray wolves and are related to foxes and jackals.
